Mission Statement

We are a Catholic educational institution in Ibadan. We provide basic holistic education to all children of school age in collaboration with the Government and other stakeholders. We use Government policies, the national curriculum, the services of our highly qualified teachers with the aid of modern facilities and equipments to produce pupils who will be able to compete effectively in the rapidly advancing technological society.

All the activities of the school are underpinned by the values of: DISCIPLINE// SELF-RELIANCE// ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE// RESPECT// MORALS// DILIGENCE.

 

We are committed to a literate society where every child has access to holistic education, becomes a person of integrity and good leader, contributing immensely to development.
